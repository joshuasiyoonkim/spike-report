Everyone stuck in silver thinks they need better aim. Some do. Most need better *habits*. Here are the five that moved my rank the fastest when I deliberately drilled them — none of which involve buying a new mouse.

## 1. Buy as a team

The single biggest low-elo leak. One person force-buying every round quietly loses you the game. Check your teammates' economy before you click "buy." A coordinated full-save beats five sad half-buys.

## 2. Use your utility *before* you die

Most silver players die with full util. Your abilities are worth nothing in your inventory. Throw the flash. Use the smoke. Even a mediocre flash that you actually use beats a perfect one you took to the grave.

## 3. Crosshair placement over flick speed

> You don't need faster reactions if the crosshair is already where the head will be.

Pre-aim common angles at head height. This one habit is worth more than a month of aim trainer.

## 4. Trade, don't avenge

When your teammate dies, the play is to trade *immediately*, not to wait three seconds and walk into the same angle solo. Stick close enough to punish.

## 5. Stop watching your own deaths

Watch your *rounds*. Why did the round lose? Usually it wasn't the death you're rewatching — it was a bad default thirty seconds earlier.

## The boring truth

None of this is flashy. All of it works. Drill one habit per week and you'll feel the climb before you see the rank up.

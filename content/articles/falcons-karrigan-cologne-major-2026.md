Seven grand finals. Seven losses. That's what Team Falcons were carrying into 2026 after m0NESY joined the roster. Reached the final of basically every event they entered, then lost it. Every time. It became a bit of a running joke, except it wasn't funny if you were NiKo.

Then they signed karrigan on April 20th, and two months later they're Major champions.

## The Missing Piece

The move made sense on paper and still felt risky. karrigan left FaZe after five years to reunite with NiKo, who has publicly called him the best IGL he's ever worked with. They won seven titles together back in 2017-2018. The chemistry was proven. But that was a different era, and Falcons were already a stacked roster that kept losing the last match of every tournament.

kyxsan got benched the same day the signing was announced. That's a cold transition for a player who helped build the team, but the results weren't there when it mattered most.

## Cologne

Falcons swept FURIA 3-0 in the grand final on June 21st. Clean. Not close. The kind of result that makes seven months of grand final losses look like a warm-up.

NiKo has his first Major. karrigan has his second, the first being PGL Antwerp 2022 with FaZe. zonic's coaching fingerprints are all over this roster.

## The Takeaway

Falcons had the firepower to win a Major for over a year. NiKo, m0NESY, TeSeS, kyousuke, that's a lineup built to close series. What they didn't have was someone to actually close them. karrigan is that player. Two months in and it's already obvious.

The superteam strategy works when you get the IGL right.
